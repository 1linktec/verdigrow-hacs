Surfaces your VerdiGrow containers into Home Assistant as native entities — the
plants growing in each container, latest metrics, and the latest photo — plus a
rich custom Lovelace card for any container or plant.

Maps HA sensors onto VerdiGrow metrics and pushes their readings on a schedule,
and keeps areas in sync between HA and VerdiGrow (import, map, or remove) — all
from the VerdiGrow Link sidebar panel.

Build area dashboards that combine plant data with your existing cameras and
irrigation. Requires a running VerdiGrow instance served over HTTPS.
